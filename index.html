Sadiya Wahid Shaikh

Kahan socha tha main ne ke, Yun baatein badhti jayegi,
Ke yun hum milte jayeinge, Aur qismat banti jayegi.

Magar ek waqaye ne, Raabta tumse banaya hai,
Ke jab se tum ko jaana hai, Na aur koi raas aaya hai.

Tumhari saadgi ke pichhe, Chhupa hai rang baaghon ka,
Ye chanchal mann tumhara, Banta hai thhandak nigaahon ka.

Yun jab tum muskurati ho, Bahaar-e-Bagh aa jaye,
Faqat hone se tere, Mehfilon mein jaan aa jaye.

"Haan bolo" bol kar tumne, Banaya mujhko bechaara.
Bhala azaad tha ye dil, Tha main befikra aawara.

Ki ab ummeed karta hun, Safar lamba ho humara.
Ke main chandaa banun tera, Bane tu mera sayyara.

Kahan socha tha main ne ke, Yun baatein badhti jayegi,
Ke yun hum milte jayeinge, Aur qismat banti jayegi.
